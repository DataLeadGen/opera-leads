Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Zakir Hussain, Opera Leads began with a simple mission: to provide businesses with the data they need to grow.

What started as a small operation has now grown into a trusted team of 10 professionals dedicated to providing accurate, reliable, and actionable data solutions. Our journey has been one of continuous innovation and improvement, always looking for ways to better serve our clients and help them achieve their goals.

We believe that quality data is the foundation of successful B2B relationships. That's why we've invested in developing cutting-edge methods and technologies to ensure our clients receive the most accurate and up-to-date information for their outreach efforts.

Today, Opera Leads stands as a leading provider of B2B data solutions, helping businesses of all sizes connect with their ideal clients and scale their operations efficiently.

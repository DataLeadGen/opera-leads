Our comprehensive email verification process includes multiple layers to ensure maximum deliverability:

  1. Syntax Check: We verify that email addresses follow the correct format (username@domain.com).
  2. Domain Verification: We check that the domain exists and has valid MX records for receiving email.
  3. Mailbox Verification: We verify that the specific mailbox exists on the domain without sending an actual email.
  4. Spam Trap Detection: We filter out known spam traps and honeypot addresses.
  5. Role-Based Email Detection: We identify and flag role-based emails (e.g., info@, sales@, support@).
  6. Disposable Email Detection: We identify temporary or disposable email addresses.
  7. Bounce History Check: When available, we check against known bounce history databases.

After verification, we categorize emails as valid, invalid, or risky, providing you with clean, high-quality data that improves deliverability rates and protects your sender reputation.
